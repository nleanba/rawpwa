Collatz Conjecture Calculator

If you take any natural Number, and repeat the following:
  • If the number is even, divide it by two.
  • If the number is odd, triple it and add one.
The Collatz Conjecture states that you will eventually end up at 1 / end up un a 4 → 2 → 1 → 4 → 2... loop.
The Collatz Conjecture could neither be proven nor beproven wrong yet. Wikipedia
